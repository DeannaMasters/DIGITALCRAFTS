Community Service

Scouts participate all year long in activities that help benefit our communities. Here are just a few projects we do:

  • Plant trees and flowers at local schools

  • Host food drives

  • Caroling at local retirement centers

  • Write thank you notes to our military overseas

  • Pick up litter at local parks and lakes

  • Place flags on military graves on Memorial Day
