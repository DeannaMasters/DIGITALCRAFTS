Cub Scouts...Need You!

Perks!

While being a Den Leader will require some effort, their are many perks as well:

  • Choose when and where the meetings will be, let it fit your schedule.

  • Plan the fun! The kids will look up to you and respect you as you are their leader, their teacher.

  • Quality time can be hard to come by. This is scheduled bonding time with your scout.

  • You will have a sense of pride when your scout earns a new advancement loop and/or rank.

  • Your scout will want to help you planning the activities and setting them up.

  • Once the other parents see you putting in the work, they are often volunteering to help wherever they can.

Picture a Yardstick:

Picture a yard stick as a representative of your child's whole life. Now visualize just the first quarter of it, that's the time we have with them during their childhood years. It is up to us, what we do with that time. Cub Scouts gives us ample opportunities to instill the values and morals we hold dear. They look up to us. And they will take pride when they say their parent is their den leader.
