The Sports!!

The 90's brought sports to all new levels. The plays, the athletes, the scandals....

History was made in this decade. People would know about these events, even if they weren't born until 20 years later!!

In basketball news:

*The Chicago Bulls had a historic repeat three-peat...twice.

Speaking of Michael Jordan....Jordan retired from basketball!! But don't worry, after he switches to baseball and ultimately doesn't succeed, he goes back to basketball. All in the 90's.

Fighting more your thing?

*In 1997, the infamous 'Bite Fight' took place. Tyson v Holyfield led to Tyson biting a part of Holyfield's ear off. OUCH!!

We must have decided that the sport wasn't violent enough

UFC was born! As was the X-Games!!

More notable names:

John Elway was considered one of the best quarterbacks of all times during this era.

Tiger Woods made a name for himself at the young age of just 21. He beat his opponent with scoring 12 under par, winning the U.S. Open.

Young Serena Williams made a name for herself by winning the US Open in tennis. She has won 22 additional Grand Slams since.

Scandals you say?

Remember Tonya and Nancy? News stories state that Tonya Harding paid others to injure Nancy Kerrigan therefore eliminating her from the Olympics. This did not go as planned and Kerrigan still won silver at the Olympics in ice skating while Harding placed eigth.

Kerri Strug, a gymnast, injured her ankle while landing a vault at the Olympics. She heard a crack, and her foot instantly went numb. Yet she was made to carry on and finish her second vault.
